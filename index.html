Who am I?

I am a student, a former athlete, a hiker, a piano player, and a sports enthusiast.

I have always been passionate about being active. I love to get outside and move. My goal is to make this into a career. I believe that moving the body is what brings optimal health, which is why I am choosing to educate myself in Public Health as well as business.

How Your Environment Affects You

Nature can influence you in ways you'd never imagine.

Growing up in Utah, I experienced all 4 seasons being drastically different. utah is also incredibly diverse in its landscapes, leading to weekends exploring different places. Seeing how nature works in harmony not only with itself, but with us if we treat it correctly, increased my desire to help others learn what I was learning. You can stay active and move outside, all while learning about what surrounds you and who you are.
